# Supply Chain Optimization: Analyzing Delivery Delays and Logistics Efficiency
Problem Statement:
In the global logistics industry, shipping delays not only increase operational costs but also significantly reduce customer satisfaction. This project aims to analyze historical supply chain data to identify bottlenecks in the delivery process and understand why certain shipments fail to meet their scheduled timelines.

Objectives:
Identify the Inefficiencies: Pinpoint the slowest shipping routes and specific geographical regions with the highest frequency of delays.

Root Cause Analysis: Investigate the factors contributing to late deliveries, such as shipping modes, product categories, or seasonal demand spikes.

Logistics Optimization: Provide data-driven insights to improve overall supply chain reliability.

Key Insights & Goals:
Alternative Route Recommendations: Suggest more efficient shipping lanes for regions identified as "High-Risk."

Vendor & Carrier Evaluation: Provide a framework for selecting logistics vendors based on their historical on-time delivery performance.

Operational Strategy: Develop recommendations for adjusting shipping schedules or modes to mitigate the risk of late arrivals.
